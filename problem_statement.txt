4. "Spend Smart" – Personalized Saving Suggestion Engine
Problem Statement
Analyze 30 days of spends and generate specific, personalized savings suggestions with estimated monthly impact.
Build Requirements

Upload CSV of transactions
Categorize expenses and compute totals
Detect overspending patterns and outliers
Generate actionable recommendations with savings estimates

User Story
"I want personalized tips so I can save at least 10–20% next month."
Sample CSV (Columns)
Date, Merchant, Amount, Category
2026-01-12, Zomato, 380, Food
2026-01-14, Uber, 240, Travel
Insight Examples

"Reduce Food ordering by 20% → Save ₹800/month"
"Travel spikes on weekends → Consider Metro card"
"Subscriptions: cancel unused services (₹X/month)"

Technical Expectations

CSV parsing and validation
Charts for category spends
Rule-based recommendation engine

Stretch Goals

What-if savings simulator (sliders)
Monthly budgeting feature
Export PDF summary

Scoring Criteria

Insight quality — 40%
Visualization — 30%
Logic — 20%
Code quality — 10%
